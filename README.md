# Reaction-time-measure
This measure human reaction time by using their web-cam . Take a scale or any object put two red mark on it at a distance between 30 cm then drop the scale vertically then simply catch it with two finger .
It's gonna measure and calculate your reaction time automatically and give you a result and allso tell you where you stand.

To start press 'S' , for stope or exit press 'Q'.
